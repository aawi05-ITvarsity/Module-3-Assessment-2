Latte

A combination of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a layer of foam

Mocha

A variant of latte made with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder added to the drink

Cappuccino

A coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and a layer of foam on top

Iced Tea

A refreshing drink made by brewing tea leaves in hot water and serving it over ice

Hot Chocolate

A rich and creamy drink made with melted chocolate and steamed milk
